Lindon

Lindon is home to many natural wonders, most of them being me and my family.




Lindon is a small town wedged between Orem and Pleasant Grove.
It does not boast many fancy things, but it does have a Walmart and a Home Depot.

I would say that its crowing jewel is the Aquatics Center, located at 60 W 60 N, Lindon, UT 84042, right next to the community center. Lindon adult residents can get it for $4, but non-resident adults can enter for $5; the slight difference doesn't deter non-residents from joining in the fun.

Lindon Aquatics Center

Lindon does sport a few restaurants, though from the following list, I have only been to one: Los Hermanos.

Los Hermanos (meaning "The Brothers", or "The Siblings") is a Mexican-style restaurant located on the corner of 400 N and State St. My wife particularly enjoys the pork chimichanga, while I like their fajitas and shrimp salad.

Here is a list of some restaurants located in Lindon, UT:

  • Los Hermanos
  • The Pizza Factory
  • The Smoking Apple
